About us

IEEE is the world's largest technical professional society dedicated to advancing technology for the benefit of humanity. IEEE brings its members access to the industry's most essential technical Information, networking opportunities, career development tools, and many other exclusive benefits. IEEE and its members inspire a global community to innovate for a better future through its highly_cited publications, confereces, technology standards and proffesional amd educational activities. There are more than 417,000 IEEE members in over 160 countries around the world.
